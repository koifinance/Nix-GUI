A Ghost node is the term used for NIX masternodes, which help secure

the network by completing special tasks on the blockchain and ensuring

quick transactions. Node holders earn NIX for providing this service.

To run a Ghost node, you will need to ‘lock’ 40,000 NIX in the node

You cannot spend those NIX while they are assigned to a node, but you can

claim them back whenever you want by stopping the node.

You will also need a Virtual Private Server (VPS)

A VPS is recommend as a Ghost node requires dedicated resources and

24x7 availability for proper operation. The VPS must have: (1) At least 2 GB

of RAM (2) At least 20 GB of storage space and (3) A static public IP address
